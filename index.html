Seif Programming Language


    
    
Tutorial

    
Welcome to Seif! Here are some basic instructions:

    
    
        
  • Drag blocks from the toolbox on the left to the workspace.
    • 
        
  • Connect blocks to create your program.
    • 
        
  • Click "Run Code" to see your program in action!
    • 
        
  • Use "Save Code" to save your project, and "Load Code" to load a saved project.
